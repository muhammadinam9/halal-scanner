Before you begin

Halal Scanner is an ingredient-screening tool to assist your own research. It is not a fatwa, a halal certification, or a substitute for guidance from a qualified Islamic scholar. Ingredient rulings — especially “doubtful” items — depend on the source, which can vary by manufacturer and region. Results may contain errors from label reading (OCR) or automated assessment. Always verify with the manufacturer and rely on recognized halal certification before consuming. You use this app at your own discretion and responsibility.
